Random Walk 3D by zb3

Hey! This is not a game (it has no objective), but a visualization of how a random walk (almost) from the inside or outside (free mode). If you want, you can actually control the construction, but you don't have to - it will just go in the random direction.

To open settings, click/tap on "Open controls". Here are some more controls:
arrows/WSAD - schedule a move (space to go straight), or move the camera in free mode
f / double click/tap  - enter/exit free mode
left drag - look around
right drag - move camera
mouse wheel - rotate camera in Z axis
long press/tap - reset camera
